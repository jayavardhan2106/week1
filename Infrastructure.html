Infrastructure at CVR College of Engineering

CVR College boasts state-of-the-art infrastructure to support academic and extracurricular activities.

  • Academic Buildings: Modern classrooms, seminar halls, and auditoriums equipped with audio-visual aids.

  • Laboratories: Well-equipped labs for various departments including computer science, electronics, mechanical, and civil engineering.

  • Library: A vast collection of books, journals, and digital resources for student and faculty use.

  • Sports Facilities: Grounds and indoor facilities for cricket, football, basketball, badminton, and more.

  • Hostels: Comfortable accommodation with all necessary amenities for boys and girls.

  • Wi-Fi Campus: High-speed internet access available across the campus.
