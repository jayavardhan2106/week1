About CVR College of Engineering

Established in 1998, CVR College of Engineering is an autonomous institution committed to excellence in engineering education, research, and innovation.

Located in Hyderabad, the college offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs and fosters an environment of learning and development.

The faculty is highly qualified and dedicated to guiding students towards achieving academic and professional success.

The college emphasizes industry collaboration, research, and community engagement to prepare students for global challenges.
